// The Problem
Startups lose great hires
to broken processes

When you're growing fast, recruitment gets messy. Candidates fall through cracks. Recruiters duplicate effort. No one has a clear picture.

📂

Scattered Spreadsheets

Every recruiter keeps their own sheet. No one knows who reached out to which candidate, or when.

🔔

Missed Follow-Ups

No reminders, no alerts. Candidates wait for days without a response and accept competing offers.

🔁

Duplicate Outreach

Two recruiters contact the same person. It looks unprofessional and wastes time for both sides.

📉

Zero Pipeline Visibility

Management can't see how many candidates are at each stage, or where the funnel is leaking.
