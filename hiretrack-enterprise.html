// The Problem
High-volume hiring breaks
spreadsheet-based ops fast

When you're screening hundreds of applicants a week — for annotation, moderation, or support roles — candidates fall through cracks, recruiters duplicate effort, and no one has a clean view of the funnel.

📂

Scattered Spreadsheets

Every recruiter keeps their own sheet. No one knows who reached out to which candidate, or when.

🔔

Missed Follow-Ups

No reminders, no alerts. Candidates wait for days without a response and drop out of the funnel.

🔁

Duplicate Outreach

Two recruiters contact the same person from different sourcing drives — it wastes time on both sides.

📉

Zero Pipeline Visibility

Ops leads can't see how many candidates are at each stage, or where the funnel is leaking.

📥

No Bulk Tools

Candidates from mass sourcing drives, vendor lists, or walk-ins get entered one row at a time.

🧪

No Skill Screening Record

Assessment or language-test scores live in someone's inbox instead of the candidate record.
